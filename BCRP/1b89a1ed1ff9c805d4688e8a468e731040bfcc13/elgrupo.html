El Grupo is a Spanish language group-based talk therapy, where we learn about and share ways to deal with the stresses of everyday life, including but not limited to anxiety, anger and depression. We learn ways to deal with these and other strong emotions and difficult life situations, as well as practice mindfulness to help change the way we think and react to difficult situations to begin with. They run Tuesdays 5:30-6:30 PM at Internal Medicine Associates Firm A (CAM Building, 17 E. 102nd Street, 7th Floor).
