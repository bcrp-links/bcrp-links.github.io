Cost

Cost to EHHOP Patient: Once your patient submits documents confirming their income, Financial Services will grant you patient Charity Care and place them on a sliding scale. Most EHHOP patients are placed on Level 1. For patients with Level 1, the Dentistry appointment will cost $15. To find out the costs for patients who are not Level 1, please contact the Accessory Referrals Manager, Kapil Mishra

    The services covered by Sinai Dental are:

  • Cleanings
  • Extractions
  • Consultations
  • Dental X-Rays
  • Fillings

Dentures are avaialable at Boriken for a discounted cost of $500 . Since this is usually difficult for most patients to afford, please get in touch with the Accessory Referrals Manager, Kapil Mishra, to work out a referral strategy.

Wait time

How long it takes patients to complete their appointment will depend on how soon the patients can submit all their documents confirming their income to Financial Services (on Average this takes 20 days). Once all documents are submitted, it takes about 6 weeks after a patient has received charity care for a dental appointment to be scheduled.
