This referral does not require charity care.

Indications: Please refer to EHHOP Women's Health Clinic if your patient has an abnormal pap smear, needs an endometrial biopsy, IUD placement, Contraception/Family Planning or pessary placement.

Routine pap smears and STI screenings can be done at the main clinic.

Six patients can be seen each month. The average wait time is 1-2 months.
