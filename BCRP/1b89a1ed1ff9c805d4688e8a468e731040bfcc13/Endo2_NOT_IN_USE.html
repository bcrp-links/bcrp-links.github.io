Access to Care Team:

  • No need to see patient unless the clinic team requests it.
  • If the patient is sent to you, please check in with the TS to make sure the referral is necessary.
  • If the referral is necessary, the patient will need to be seen by Financial Services to obtain charity care before their appointment can be scheduled.
  • Please find mutually convenient time during THAT week when both you and the patient can go to financial services together.
  • Stress that the patient will not be able to keep their specialty appointment unless they keep their financial services appointment.
  • Also remind the patient that you are taking time out of your very busy medical school schedule to help them with their appointment.
  • In the sign out email to the Referrals Chair and ACT coordinator, please indicate when your patient's FS appointment is
  • Also update the ACT/Referrals problem list with this information.
  • If you are unable to accompany your patient that week, find out three times during the week when your patient will be available to go to their appointment between 8AM and 5PM M-F. Email these times to the Referrals Chair and the Financial Services Manager.
