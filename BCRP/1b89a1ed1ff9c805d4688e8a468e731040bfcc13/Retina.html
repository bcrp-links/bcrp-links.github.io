Once your patient has charity care, please contact the Emerging Referrals Manager. The Emerging referrals manager will give you directions on how to schedule your patient for their specialty referral appointment.

Cost

The cost of an appointment for patients with charity care level 1 is $15. Appointments will be more expensive for patients with a higher charity care level. For these prices, contact Kristen Ng.

Outpatient Retina appointments cost ~300 in New York out of pocket.

Appointment costs by charity care level are as follows:

  • CCL1 - $15
  • CCL2 - $18
  • CCL3 - $36
  • CCL4 - $90
  • CCL5 - $108
  • CCL6 - $135
  • CCL7 - $180

Wait time

It takes about 6 weeks after a patient has received charity care for a retina appointment to be scheduled. Urgent referrals can be expedited. Contact Kristen Ng if the referral is urgent and needs to be expedited.
