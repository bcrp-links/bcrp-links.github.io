Exercise Group

Back

EHHOP has two weekly exercise offerings: a walking group that meets at 10AM and strolls around the reservoir in Central Park (~45 mins) and a fitness group that meets in one of the patient waiting rooms at 11AM. The goal of the classes is to create a social space that encourages relationships between patients and student volunteers that will increase incentive for our patients to exercise regularly.
