EHHOP clinicians have access to a telephone translator service called Pacific Translators. To use the service with a non-English speaking patient in clinic, please do the following:

  1. Using your cell phone call 1-800-264-1552.
    • Give the operator the following information:
    • Access code: 828099 (Mount Sinai Medical Center)
    • Language: Spanish (or whatever else you need)
    • Main Hospital or FPA: Main Hospital
    • Name:
    • Life number:
    • Department name: Main Hospital
    • Department code: 684
  2. Once you are connected to a translator, place your phone on speaker and start talking to your patient!
