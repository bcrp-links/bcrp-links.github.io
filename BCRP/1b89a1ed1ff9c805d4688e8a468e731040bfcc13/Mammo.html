Sinai Breast Resources provides EHHOP with free Mammograms and Breast Ultrasounds.

The protocol for Breast Ultrasounds and mammograms is the same.The same form needs to be filled out for both.

Please note that further intervention following an abnormal mammogram will be  coordinated by Mount Sinai Breast Health Resource Program. For example, once an EHHOP  patient establishes with this program, EHHOP clinicians do not need to put in any  referrals for follow-up imaging or biopsies. This should all be taken care of by their  social worker and case managers. Should you have questions, please contact the Referrals team.

Senior Clinician

The referrals team will schedule a breast imaging appointment for your patient, and  follow them through the rest of the referral.
