  • Unstable angina
  • Congestive heart failure
  • Cardiac ultrasound
  • Need for stress test
  • Need for cardiac catheterization

    Relative Indications

  • Multiple CAD risk factors (age, family history, diabetes, HTN, AAA, smoking)"
