    Cost

    Cost to EHHOP Patient: Once your patient submits documents confirming their  income, Financial Services will grant you patient Charity Care and place them on a  sliding scale. Most EHHOP patients are placed on Level 1. For patients with Level 1,  the Pulmonology appointment will cost $15. To find out the costs for patients  who are not Level 1, please contact the Emerging Referrals Manager, Kristen Ng

    Cost to Society: Outpatient pulmonology appointment cost ~300 in new york  out of pocket.

    Wait time

    How long it takes patients to complete their appointment will depend on how soon the patients can submit all their documents confirming their income to Financial Services (on Average this takes 20 days). Once all documents are submitted, it takes about 3 weeks after a patient has received charity care for a pulmonology appointment to be scheduled.
